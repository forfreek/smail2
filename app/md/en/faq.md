## trashmail.space FAQ

### What is trashmail.space?
trashmail.space is a disposable inbox for low-risk signups, verification messages, and short-lived email checks. It is designed for receiving messages quickly without using your primary mailbox.

### Is trashmail.space the same as smail pro or smailpro?
No. trashmail.space is an independent service. The name overlap is a search confusion issue, not a product relationship.

### How fast can I get an address?
Immediately. Open the homepage and generate an address without account setup.

### Do I need to register?
No. trashmail.space works without account registration.

### How long are messages stored?
Messages are kept for up to 24 hours, then removed. Treat the inbox as temporary access, not as storage.

### Can I use it for OTP or verification links?
Yes. OTP codes, signup confirmations, and one-time verification links are some of the main use cases.

### Why did a verification email not arrive?
Common reasons:

- Sender-side delay
- Provider blocks temporary domains
- Typo in the address

Refresh the inbox first. If nothing appears, resend from the source website and confirm that the address was entered correctly. Some providers do not deliver to disposable domains at all.

### Can I send emails from trashmail.space?
No outbound workflow should be assumed. trashmail is built for receiving messages, and sending or replying may be unavailable.

### Is this inbox private?
No. A disposable inbox is not the same as a personal authenticated mailbox. Do not use it for sensitive data, private documents, or anything that depends on long-term access.

### Can I keep one address forever?
No. Addresses are disposable by design. If you need stable ownership of the mailbox, use a permanent provider.

### Is trashmail.space suitable for important accounts?
No. Do not use it for financial, legal, work, government, or recovery-critical accounts.

### What if emails disappear?
That can happen when the retention window expires or cleanup runs. If a message matters, move the information you need out of the inbox while it is still available.

### Is trashmail.space free?
Yes. It is available free for temporary inbox use.
