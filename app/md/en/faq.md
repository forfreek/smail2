## trashmail.space FAQ

### What is trashmail.space?
trashmail.space is a temporary email service and temp mail generator for disposable inboxes. It is built for low-risk sign-ups, verification codes, and one-off email reception.

### Is trashmail.space the same as smail pro or smailpro?
No. trashmail.space is an independent service and is not affiliated with other similarly named temp mail websites.

### How fast can I get an address?
Immediately. Open the homepage and generate an address in one tap.

### Do I need to register?
No. trashmail.space works without account registration.

### How long are messages stored?
Messages are kept for up to 24 hours, then automatically removed.

### Can I use it for OTP or verification links?
Yes. That is one of the most common use cases.

### Why did a verification email not arrive?
Common reasons:

- Sender-side delay
- Provider blocks temporary domains
- Typo in the address

Try refreshing the inbox and resending from the source website.

### Can I send emails from trashmail.space?
The service is primarily for receiving messages. Sending or replying may be unavailable.

### Is this inbox private?
Temporary inboxes are not equivalent to personal authenticated mailboxes. Do not store sensitive data here.

### Can I keep one address forever?
No. Addresses are disposable by design and may expire.

### Is trashmail.space suitable for important accounts?
No. Use a long-term secure provider for financial, legal, work, and recovery-critical accounts.

### What if emails disappear?
This is expected in temporary systems when retention expires or cleanup rules run.

### Is trashmail.space free?
Yes, trashmail.space is designed as a free temporary mailbox utility.
